Huge Sale of Used Books, Media, and Art on the 2nd Saturday and Sunday of Every Month!

  • Over 50,000 gently used books, movies, magazines, and more!
  • Sourced from generous donations and former library materials.
  • No sales tax and 100% of your purchase benefits Palo Alto Libraries.
  • Buy FOPAL gift certificates in $5 denominations.
  • Our super friendly sales people are helpful and happy to assist!

FOPAL Prices

  • Hardcover books: $1.00 each
  • Paperback books: $0.50 each
  • Children's books: $0.25 each

FOPAL Sale Locations and Featured Items

FOPAL's sale is located in 3 rooms at Cubberley Community Center, 4000 Middlefield Road, Palo Alto, CA 94303. Directions and Maps

  • Main Room- features non-fiction & fiction books in all genres, CDs, DVDs, games, and puzzles.
  • Children's Book Room- has books, CDs, and DVDs for preschool through early teens, and books for parents and teachers, games, puzzles, and toys.
  • Bargain Room- offers amazing deals on hardcovers and paperbacks, DVDs, CDs, audiobooks, records, children's books, and former library books. On Sundays, all items are 50% off or $5/bag.

Saturday Sale Entry Procedure

  • Maximum of 80 customers are allowed in the Main Room due to health and safety issues.
  • The Ticket System will be implemented when there is an excess of people waiting in line outside.
  • The 12-Book Limit is enforced during the first hour of the sale.

How the Ticket System Works

  1. Numbered tickets are given out from 8am to 11am at the Main Room.
  2. The number on the ticket corresponds to your spot number in line.
  3. Reclaim your spot in line before your number gets called for entry or you will lose your place in line.
  4. If you arrive after 11am, then wait at the end of the North Entrance line.

12-Book Limit

  1. Select only 12 books during the first hour of the sale.
  2. Pay or ask the cashier to hold them until you're ready to pay for all your purchases.
  3. Exit at the East door.
  4. Re-join the line at the North Entrance for re-entry into the Main Room.
  5. Limit of 12 books at a time will continue past noon if a line remains outside.
