Huge Sale of Used Books, Media, and Art on the 2nd Saturday and Sunday of Every Month!

  • Over 50,000 gently used books, movies, magazines, and more!
  • Sourced from generous donations and weeded library materials.
  • No sales tax and 100% of your purchase goes to supporting our local libraries.
  • Buy FOPAL gift certificates in $5 denominations.
  • Super friendly sales people are happy to assist!

FOPAL Prices

  • Hardcover books: $1.00 each
  • Paperback books: $0.50 each
  • Children's books: $0.25 each

FOPAL Sale Locations and Featured Items

FOPAL's sale is located in 3 rooms at Cubberley Community Center, 4000 Middlefield Road, Palo Alto, CA 94303. Directions

  • Main Room- features non-fiction & fiction books in all genres, CDs, DVDs, games, and puzzles.
  • Children's Book Room- has books, CDs, and DVDs for preschool through early teens, and books for parents and teachers, games, puzzles, and toys.
  • Bargain Room- offers amazing deals on hardcovers and paperbacks, DVDs, CDs, audiobooks, records, children's books, and ex-library books. On Sundays, all items are 50% off or $5/bag.

Entry Procedure for the Saturday Sale

  • Only 160 customers are allowed in the Main Room due to health and safety issues.
  • The Ticket System will be implemented when there is an excess of people waiting in line outside.
  • The 12-Book limit will be enforced during the first hour of the sale.

How the Ticket System Works

  1. Numbered tickets are given out from 8:00 - 11:00 am at the Main Room.
  2. The number on the ticket corresponds to your spot number in line.
  3. Reclaim your spot in line before your number gets called for entry or you will lose your place in line.
  4. If you arrive after 11:00 am, then join at the end of the line.

12-Book Limit

  1. Select only 12 books during the first hour of the sale.
  2. Pay or ask cashier to hold until you're ready to pay for all your purchases.
  3. Exit at the east door.
  4. To re-enter the sale, re-join the line at the north door.
  5. Limitation of 12 books at a time will continue past 12:00 pm if a line remains outside.
